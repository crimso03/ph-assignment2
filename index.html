Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

The curriculum focuses on developing basic language skills, early math concepts, and physical coordination through play-based activities

Elementary School

Students are introduced to core subjects such as reading, writing, mathematics, science, and social studies with an emphasis on building foundational knowledge and critical thinking

Middle (10-16) Years

The curriculum offers a deeper exploration of subjects, including advanced math, science, language arts, and social studies, with a focus on independent learning and critical analysis
